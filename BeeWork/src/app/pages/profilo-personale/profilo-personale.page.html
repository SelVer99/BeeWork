Bio Paul Yeboah (born 4 August 1992), known as Bello FiGo, is a Ghanaian YouTuber and singer known in Italy for his numerous songs in which he often parodies different topics like racism, sexism and politics that often caused controversy and debate in Italy about his social, political and cultural role because of the way he talks about these topics.
